Yearbook club at EIPACA

Throughout the school years S2-S6 (Grades 7-11, 2013-present), I have been part of the yearbook club. The yearbook club is responsible for the organising, designing, printing, selling and distributing of the school's yearbook. This yearbook contains not only class photos and individual student photos, but also all of the school's events throughout the year. The format of the yearbook changes every year, along with a theme (For example - No Limits -> 2015-16).
Each meeting, very memeber of the yearbook club is given a task to perform (for example, taking photos of an event, creating a page for a class, asking for missing photos).
In 2015, I was the head editor of the yearbook. I was in charge of distributing tasks to the other students, following milestones, and proofreading completed yearbook pages.
In 2017 (S6 = Grade 11), I will be in charge of the yearbook club along with 2 other classmates. We will need to schedule the club's meetings, decide/vote on any format-related changes, and create a list of milestones/dates for the yearbook tasks to be completed by.
