International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE)

In 2016/17, when I was in S5 (equivalent of grade 10 in the Canadian Curriculum), our class was given the option to, alongside of regular school work, pass IGCSE exams on any available subjects. These are British exams offered to students studying outside the UK in the equivalent grade of 10 in Canada.
At the beginning of S5, I signed up for 4 exams:
  • Physics
    • Paper 1P Summer 2017
    • Paper 2P Summer 2017
  • ICT
    • Written Paper Summer 2017
    • Practical Paper Summer 2017
  • Mathematics
    • Paper 3H Summer 2017
    • Paper 4H Summer 2017
  • French
    • Paper 1 Writing Summer 2017
    • Paper 2 Listening Summer 2017
    • Paper 3 Speaking Summer 2017

Most IGCSE exams are composed of 2 papers, which the students pass on different days, except for languages, where one is required to pass 2 papers followed by a recorded oral conversation.
I prepared throughout the course of the school year, and successfully passed the exams in June/July 2017. I viewed my results online and recieved A* in all 4 exams that I took.

Certificate can be provided upon request.
