Diplôme d'études en langue française (DELF) A2 Level

The DELF, translated as Diploma in French Language Studies is a certification of french language abilities of non-native french speakers. It can not be passed after you have resided in France for more than 2 years.
This means that I had to complete the delf when on my second year in France, learning french, in the 2011/2012 school year (CM2, or grade 5).

The exam has different levels of difficulty, from A1 to B2.

I recieved this certification for the A2 Level.
